Welcome to the classic game of Hangman! Test your vocabulary 
and save the stickman before it is too late.

🎮 HOW TO PLAY
------------------------------------------------------------
1. Start the game and choose "Game" from the main menu.
2. The computer will randomly select a secret word and display 
   it as a row of blank underscores (_).
3. Guess one letter at a time to uncover the hidden word.


❌ THE RULES OF GUESSING & ERRORS
------------------------------------------------------------
• ✅ Correct Guess: If the letter is in the word, it will 
  reveal its correct position(s).
• ❌ Incorrect Guess: If the letter is not in the word, it is 
  an error. An extra part of the gallows/hangman will be drawn.
• ⚠️ Already Guessed: Guessing a letter you have already found 
  counts as an error!
• ⚠️ Empty Input: Pressing Enter without typing a letter 
  counts as an error!


🏆 WINNING & LOSING
------------------------------------------------------------
• 🎉 You Win: Correctly guess all the letters to reveal the 
  complete word before the drawing is finished.
• 💀 Game Over: Accumulating 10 total errors completes the 
  drawing, resulting in a defeat and revealing the secret word.


📊 GAME MODES & MENUS
------------------------------------------------------------
• Game: Launch a new puzzle and try to solve the hidden word.
• Rules: The mode provides players with a quick guide to the game's mechanics, winning conditions, and menu navigation.
• Highscores: View your lifetime total tracking of Victories 
  and Defeats.
• Settings: Update your player Name or switch the game Language 
  and word banks.
• Exit: Safely close the game application.
